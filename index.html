Set beneath Gwydir Forest and opposite the River Llugwy, Glenwood is a family-run guest house within easy reach of Betws-y-Coed, walking routes and North Wales adventures.

Come back to comfortable en-suite rooms, a garden made for slowing down and hosts who know the area.
